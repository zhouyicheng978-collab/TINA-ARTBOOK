EMBERS
IN THE SEA

海水燃烬

“I invite you to take part in this dream,
and we are all co-creators of this dreamscape.”

“邀请你，参与这场梦，
而我们，都是这场梦境的共创者。”

“Lost souls fill this building, wandering all day long,
longing for revenge with broken hearts.
Before the night comes,
they wish to share some secrets with you.”

“这幢楼里住着许多失落的恶魂，终日游荡着，
怀着破碎的心，渴望复仇。
在夜晚来临之前，
他们想透露一些秘密给你听。”

Fortune favors the brave.

天佑勇者

命运总是眷顾勇敢的人。

Do not swear by the moon, for it is fickle. 不要指着月亮起誓，它自己都变化无常。

We trust that the moon shall guide us.
We shall be intimate with darkness.

相信月亮的指引
拥抱黑暗。

Images That Keep Their Secrets

画册被整理成一个缓慢展开的私人档案，像夜里的房间、信件、证词和未完成的梦。

✦

And so, let go.
When the dream comes to an end,
stop trying so hard to distinguish the real from the unreal.
Stop insisting on naming every love and every pain.
Let everything remain in memory, softly blurred.
Images speak in silence,
yet they leave behind whatever you wish to keep.
Enter the dream. Lose yourself. Leave.
Bless the brave.
Until we meet again.

于是放下。
当梦境终结时，
不再用力去辨别真实与虚幻，
不再执意表达爱或痛。
开始允许一切模糊地留存在记忆中。
图像无言，会留下你想留下的东西。
入梦，迷失，离开。
天佑勇者，后会有期。
